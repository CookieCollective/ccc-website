Michał (Wojna) Liberak - electronic music and multimedia producer / performer. Audiovisual manipulator. A humanoid in disguise. During the warm season, can be found near meadows and forests. In winter, he appears mainly in bunkers, vacant buildings and devastated basements. Sleeps in an inflatable coffin at the edge of the cemetery by the sea. Likes to travel. Generates sounds and images out of machines. Supporter of the anti-fascist, teknopunk movement and sound system culture.
