My name is Mari Moura. I’m a black Brazilian performer artist interested in the relationship between body and technology in tangible space and cyberspace. I'm Artist and Researcher in Performance Art, activist for the presence of black women in Art and Technology. I'm interested in the relationship between art, the body, algorithmic patterns and technology in tangible space and cyberspace, together with visual arts, live coding and body notation. I'm PhD in Visual Arts in the Art and Technology research line at UNB, with a sandwich doctorate in Paris V at the Institut des sciences du sport-santé de Paris V Me chamo Joenio Marques da Costa, sou estudande de Doutorado em Ciência da Computação na Universidade Federal da Bahia(UFBA), trabalho atualmente como Engenheiro de Software para Pesquisa (Research Software Engineer) na pataforma CorTexT, sou também apoiador do Software Livre (Free Software), desenvolvedor Debian, instrutor The Carpentries, embaixador do Software Heritage e co-criador junto com Mari Moura do laboratório 4two.art de pesquisa, criação e experimentação em arte, ciência e tecnologia.
