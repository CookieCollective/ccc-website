Stella Jacob is a multimedia artist based in Paris,passionate about immersive media. Graduated from Gobelins school in Game Design, she previously worked for five years in the Virtual Reality field. From trash-made costumes to video games, her work is about communities, either online or offline. Stella Jacob est une artiste multimédia basée à Paris, passionnée par les médias immersifs.
