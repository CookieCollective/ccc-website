8rix is an artist from Paris who uses his phone to make chiptune music, melted with everyday sounds he records on-the-fly.
As a music enthusiast he likes to put some hip-hop, grime, ambient or latino influence among four-on-the-floor tracks. His universe is an unexpected assembly of station announcements, birds singing, reverberated voices, distorted and pitched sounds, children construction games, where trains go on their rails endlessly.
When on stage he works with LimeLimeWire, a multi-gifted artist and ally whose hypnotizing visuals perfectly fit his music.
